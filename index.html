Everything You Need in One Distro

Moh-OS combines the power of Debian with carefully curated tools for developers, competitive programmers, and digital creators. Choose between GNOME and KDE Plasma desktops, both optimized for performance and aesthetics.

Key Features

Dual Desktop Environments

Enjoy both GNOME and KDE Plasma, meticulously configured for optimal workflow and visual appeal. Switch between them at login.

Competitive Programming Ready

Pre-installed with VS Code, PyCharm, Vim, Neovim, and compilers for C++, C, Python, and Pascal. Start coding immediately.

Creative Suite

DaVinci Resolve for video editing, Krita for digital painting, and GIMP for photo manipulation - all configured and ready.

Gaming & Streaming

Steam with Proton support for gaming and OBS Studio pre-configured for streaming and recording your sessions.

Browser Choice

Brave for privacy, Chrome for compatibility, and Firefox for customization - all updated and ready to use.

Optimized Performance

Carefully tuned for responsiveness with essential drivers pre-installed for both Intel/AMD and NVIDIA hardware.
